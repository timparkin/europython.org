PyCon UK Location

Birmingham Conservatoire, the venue for the conference, is located in the city centre close to all the facilities you'd wish:

  • Restaurants
  • Shops
  • Railway and Coach Stations
  • Museums and Art Galleries
  • Theatres
  • Symphony Hall
  • Night Clubs
  • Tourist Attractions
  • Hotels

All of these are within a short walking distance, yes we mean this, no more than 1 to 10 minutes.
